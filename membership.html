Membership

Telangana Association of Greater San Antonio will have only one class of membership; Life Membership. Life Membership dues are $100 for individuals or a family. A Life Member will have 2 votes if married (One for Member and one for his/her spouse); 1 vote if single.

Qualification of Members

Any Person who is above 18 years of age and is born in the Telangana region and has interests of Telangana state to their heart would qualify for membership.

Fees and Dues

Life Members pay a onetime membership fee of $100. They will not have any dues annually. Membership dues are not refundable under any circumstances.

Please refer to TAGSA By Laws for additional details.
