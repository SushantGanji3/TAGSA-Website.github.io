Donations

Telangana Association of Greater San Antonio(TAGSA) is a non-profit organization and all donations are used exclusively for cultural, health, educational and charitable purposes within the meaning of Section 501 (c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, or the corresponding section of any future Federal tax code.

How to donate

Donations to TAGSA can be made by:

  1. Donate and Pay membership fees online using Debit or Credit card using TAGSA Square Payment Portal: http://bit.ly/donatetagsa
  2. Contacting president@tagsa.org via email and pledge your support. One of our volunteers will arrange for donation pickup or provide ACH details or Paypal to transfer electronically and securely whichever is most convenient for you.
  3. TAGSA as a registered non-profit organization, is setup to receive donations through Benevity donation platform. This platform is used by many organizations that offer matching donations as well. This platform can be used to donate regularly or onetime through easy payroll deduction. Find out from your employer’s HR department if it is available.
  4. TAGSA is also registered with Amazon Smiles donation program. This is another way to help TAGSA support COVID relief efforts while you are making purchases online at Amazon.com or Amazon mobile app. This does not change price of the product instead Amazon donates parts of proceeds it receives from sales to non-profits like TAGSA. Register and Select “Telangana Association of Greater San Antonio” as your favorite charity at smile.amazon.com. Please visit Amazon.com to setup TAGSA as your favorite charity while making purchases on amazon.com
